Our Story

Who We Are

The Joel Foundation is an NGO dedicated to giving back to society, with a special focus on supporting motherless babies and widows within the community.

  • 💛

    Community First

    Everything we do puts vulnerable community members at the centre of our work.

  • 🌍

    UK–Nigeria Bridge

    Connecting UK resources with Nigerian communities where they matter most.

Our Mission

The Joel Foundation is an NGO dedicated to giving back to society, with a special focus on supporting motherless babies and widows, and improving access to healthcare in rural Nigeria.

Our Vision

A world where every child has access to care, every widow has community support, and every rural clinic has the equipment it needs.

Our Programmes

How We Give Back

Three focused programmes that create direct, measurable impact in Nigerian communities.

New Programme
🏥

Medical Equipment Drive

We source donated medical equipment from the UK health sector — including hospitals and NHS trusts — and ship them directly to Nigerian rural hospitals and health centres. From diagnostic tools to basic surgical equipment, we bridge the resource gap that limits care in underserved communities.

Quarterly Food Giveaways

Every quarter, The Joel Foundation carries out targeted food item giveaways to selected beneficiary groups in Nigeria — with a special focus on motherless babies, widows, and elderly community members who depend on community support to survive.

Medical Equipment Drive

Pledge Medical Equipment

Do you work in the UK health sector, or know of surplus medical equipment that could change lives? We handle shipping, logistics, and distribution to verified Nigerian rural health centres.

  • Diagnostic equipment — stethoscopes, BP monitors, thermometers
  • Surgical tools and consumable medical supplies
  • Maternity and neonatal care equipment
  • Basic lab equipment for rural clinics
  • Wheelchairs, crutches, and mobility aids
